About

Paste & Go是由4個輔仁大學資工系的學生所做的課堂專案，它可以讓你快速分享資料，只要貼上你想要分享的資料並自訂縮短連結，便可以將你的資料分享給任何人！

這個專案使用了AJAX, PHP, Bootstrap, MSSQL以及reCAPTCHA等工具。

Paste & Go is a school project made by four computer science major students in Fu Jen University. It lets you quickly share any information you want. Just post the info and customize your url link, you can share it anywhere! This project used tools such as AJAX, PHP, Bootstrap, MSSQL and reCAPTCHA.
